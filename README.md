# Home Teams Win More Often Than Visitors in English Football
## This project uses the Fjelstul English Football Database, a public dataset created by Josh Fjelstul and hosted on GitHub. The database contains records of soccer matches played in the English Football league and Premier League from the 1888089 season through the 2021-22 season. It includes information such as the season, teams, match results, scores, and league division. The dataset appears to be trustworthy because it was creaed by a researcher who documents the database and provides a codebook explaining each variable. The information comes from official football records and historical league results rather than surveys or opinions. Although it is not produced by a government agency, it is transparent about how the data was compiled and is widely available for anyone to inspect. One limitation is that the dataset only contains English professional football leagues, so the results cannot be generalized to other countries or competitions. Because the data spans more than 130 years, changes in league structure, rules, and the number of teams may also affect the comparisons across different time periods. In addition, the dataset records match outcomes but does not include factors such as player injuries, weather, or referee decisions that may have influenced the results.
